About Us

Nirbhaugatka Academy is committed to preserving and teaching Gatka, the traditional Sikh martial art that combines agility, discipline, and respect. We provide authentic training to students of all ages in a safe and encouraging environment.

Our Training Programs

Beginner Gatka

Learn the fundamentals, including basic stances, footwork, and weapon handling.

Intermediate Gatka

Develop advanced skills, combinations, demo and controlled sparring techniques.

Advanced Gatka

Train like a professional with high-level combat strategies, performance choreography, and tournaments.
